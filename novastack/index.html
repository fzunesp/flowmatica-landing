Frequently Asked Questions

Can I really run this on my own computer?

Yes! NovaStack runs on any computer — Windows, Mac, or Linux. Even an old laptop or small dedicated machine works great. No cloud accounts required.

Can multiple people access it?

Yes! Install it on a computer on your network and everyone connects to it. You control who sees what — admin, HR, or regular user. No per-user fees. Ever.

Can I migrate from my current tools?

Yes! NovaStack includes import/export tools for contacts, deals, and invoices. Migration guides for popular platforms are in development.

What if my computer crashes?

Everything is stored in a single database file. Back it up to anywhere (USB drive, external hard drive, or wherever you like). Restore takes 30 seconds. It's just a file.

What modules are included?

Dashboard (Command Center), Companies Hub, CRM (contacts + deals + visual board), Invoices, Products, Intake Management, HR Operations, Tasks, Settings, and a built-in Help Center.

What are the minimum system requirements?

Any modern computer: 2GB RAM minimum (4GB recommended), 500MB disk space, any modern CPU. Even an old laptop or small dedicated machine works great.
